## üìã Engagement Models

### 1. Fixed-Price Implementation
**Best For:** Well-defined scope, standard deployment

**Typical Inclusions:**
- Solution deployment
- Integration with 2-3 systems
- User training
- Go-live support
- 3-month warranty

**Typical Cost:** ¬£30k-100k depending on solution

---

### 2. Time & Materials
**Best For:** Complex/uncertain scope, iterative approach

**Typical Day Rates:**
- Solution Architect: ¬£800-1,200
- Technical Lead: ¬£600-900
- Developer: ¬£450-700
- Business Analyst: ¬£500-750
- Change Manager: ¬£600-850

---

### 3. Managed Service
**Best For:** Ongoing operational support

**Typical Monthly Fee:** ¬£2k-10k depending on:
- Number of users
- SLA requirements
- Support hours
- Infrastructure management

---

### 4. Outcome-Based
**Best For:** Focus on results, shared risk/reward

**Payment Triggers:**
- Successful go-live
- User adoption milestones
- Benefits realization targets
- Performance metrics achieved

## ðŸ’¡ Tips for Accurate ROI

1. **Be Conservative** - Underestimate savings, overestimate costs
2. **Include Hidden Costs** - Training, change management, ongoing support
3. **Consider Soft Benefits** - Improved satisfaction, staff morale, reputation
4. **Account for Gradual Adoption** - Full benefits may take 6-12 months
5. **Factor in Data Quality** - Poor data may delay benefits
6. **Include Opportunity Cost** - What could staff do with freed time?

## üéì Key Learnings & Insights

### Competitive Landscape Insights

1. **AI Adoption Gap is Huge**
   - Only 35-40% of UK public sector software has AI capabilities
   - 89% of our solutions include LLM/AI
   - 2-3 year first-mover advantage possible

2. **UK-Specific Positioning Works**
   - Most competitors are US-focused (Instrumentl, Workday, Tableau)
   - Our UK-specific positioning differentiates immediately
   - Integrated with Civica, NEC, Capita = 70%+ of market

3. **Government Investment is Real**
   - ¬£34M fraud detection investment (Apr 2025)
   - ¬£2-3B annual digital transformation budgets
   - Post-pandemic AI adoption accelerating

4. **Cost Matters in Public Sector**
   - 40-60% cost savings vs. legacy vendors = powerful messaging
   - Replacement costs (¬£500k-2M) create stickiness
   - ROI payback in weeks/months, not years

5. **Vertical Specialization Beats Horizontal**
   - Deep housing expertise > shallow everything expertise
   - 13 housing solutions create platform effect
   - Bundling 3-5 solutions increases value 30-50%

### Market Maturity Insights

| Category | Maturity | AI Adoption | Competitive Intensity | Best Approach |

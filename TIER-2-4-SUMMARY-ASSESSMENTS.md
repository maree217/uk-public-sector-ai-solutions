### 2. RECRUITMENT SCREENING ENGINE

**Tier:** 2 (Strong Contender)

**Category:** Human Resources

**Market Opportunity:** £280M (5.4M public sector employees)

**Problem:**
- 400k+ vacancies annually in UK public sector
- Manual CV screening takes 20-40 hours per role
- Unconscious bias in hiring decisions
- High cost per hire (£3,000-8,000)

**Solution:**
- AI-powered CV screening and candidate ranking
- Bias detection and mitigation built-in
- Shortlist generation in minutes
- Integration with HR systems (iTrent, ResourceLink, Workday)

**Competitive Landscape:**
- **Workday** (£100-300k/year, enterprise focus)
- **CVViZ** (£5-20k/year, generic CV screening)
- **Brainner** (£3-15k/year, UK-based but new)
- **Manual processes** (expensive, biased)

**Our Advantages:**
1. Public sector expertise (knows iTrent, ResourceLink)
2. Bias mitigation features
3. Smaller organizations (vs. Workday enterprise positioning)
4. Faster deployment (weeks vs. months)

**Competitive Positioning:**
- Target: Mid-size councils/housing/charities (500-2,000 staff)
- Price: £5,000-10,000/year
- Value prop: "Screen candidates 10x faster while eliminating bias"

**Year 1 Revenue Potential:** £1M annually (100+ organizations at £10k average)

**Win Scenarios:**
- Organizations struggling with hiring timelines
- Equality/DEI-focused procurement (bias detection)
- High-volume recruiting (10+ roles/year)

## TIER 4: NEEDS DIFFERENTIATION (8 Solutions)

Solutions requiring strategic repositioning or enhanced differentiation to compete effectively.

### Tier 4 Analysis

| Solution | Challenge | Market Maturity | Strategy |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|----------|
| **Data Query Assistant** | Power BI/Tableau dominate (90%+ share) | Hyper-mature | Vertical specialization or Power BI add-on |
| **Business Intelligence Assistant** | Generic BI tools well-established | Mature | Target small orgs, partner with Tableau |
| **Demand Forecasting Engine** | Excel/Python widely used for forecasting | Mature | Specialize by sector (housing, social care) |
| **Performance Dashboard Generator** | Power BI automation already exists | Mature | Target non-technical users |
| **Predictive Analytics Engine** | Generic analytics platforms cover space | Mature | Domain specialization (housing, councils) |
| **Health & Safety Compliance AI** | Existing H&S software vendors strong | Mature | Focus on small-medium organizations |
| **Audit Evidence Collector** | Internal audit processes vary widely | Fragmented | Specialize by sector or regulator requirement |
| **Waste Collection Assistant** | Citizens app + Civica CRM existing solutions | Nascent | Bundle as platform play, not standalone |

### Tier 4 Strategic Recommendation

**Don't abandon these solutions.** Instead:

1. **Vertical Specialization** - Position as specialized solutions within verticals
   - Data Query Assistant → "Housing-specific analytics" not "generic BI"
   - Demand Forecasting → "Social care demand prediction" not generic

2. **Partnership Model** - Become extensions to dominant platforms
   - BI Assistant → Microsoft Power BI add-on
   - Data Query → Integration with Google Data Studio

3. **Niche Targeting** - Focus on underserved segments
   - Audit Evidence → Smaller charities (big orgs use Deloitte)
   - H&S Compliance → Small housing providers

4. **Platform Bundling** - Combine with Tier 1-2 solutions
   - Performance Dashboard + Arrears Predictor
   - Waste Collection Assistant + Citizen Service Assistant

**Year 1 Revenue Potential (Tier 4):** £2-4M (focus on bundling with higher tiers)
